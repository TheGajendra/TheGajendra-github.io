Our Story

Founded in 2024, myBus was born out of a simple yet ambitious idea: to create a reliable and user-friendly platform where travelers could easily book bus tickets anytime, anywhere. What started as a small team with a passion for travel and technology has grown into one of the leading bus booking platforms in Rajasthan.
